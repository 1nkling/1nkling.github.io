When I step on the court, I expect the rules of basketball to apply.  When I head home, I don’t expect to hear a whistle or see anyone shooting free throws.  Rationality is a ruleset that has grown far beyond its arena.  It is useful in aspects of my life, and a fair bit more than the rules of basketball.  But attempting to live life rationally is about as useful as attempting to live using a FIBA ruleset.  At the same time, it is so pervasive that I think that there is no other way.

If I am unable to incorporate irrational people and irrational behaviors into my life, I have two choices.  I can reject everything that is irrational, or I can expand my philosophy to allow for the irrational.  Rejecting irrationality starts a war within me - the irrational world trying to break in, and my irrational heart trying to break out.  I can only take so much of this before I have no choice to pursue the alternative.

Language belies the prejudice we hold against the “ir”-rational.  Rationally there is “wrong” or “right”.  But to sincerely pursue the irrational is not only to consider something “incorrect” with the same weight as something that is “correct”, but to relinquish my entire rational framework of understanding altogether.  But what else is there you ask?  Something beyond concepts.

In the beginning it is feeling for shapes in the dark.  But in my heart I’ve always felt the push and pull.  I had just chosen to ignore it over the guidance of my mind.  Everything has its place in the world.  It is only rationality that says otherwise.  But what of murderers?  What of cruelty and violence?  They too have their place.  But not as a means to an end.  Not because “some people are just born that way”.  And certainly not because they are normal behaviors.  In fact they are an expression of a deep, collective psychic disturbance.

天地之間，其猶橐籥乎？The spaces between heaven and earth - are they not like a bellows?  The wind blowing through the trees creates music, low notes and high notes.  The only trap, dear reader, I would caution you against is believing you have accounted for the irrational by thinking you understand it.  In reality, there is nothing you can meaningfully understand conceptually.  There is only one thing you can understand, and you would not be able to explain it anyways.
